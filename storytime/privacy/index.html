Privacy Policy

Last updated: 23 July 2026

The short version: Storytime does not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data. Everything stays on your device.

Overview

This privacy policy explains how Storytime ("the app"), made by Compution Ltd, handles your information when you use the app.

Data Collection

Storytime does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Specifically:

  • The app does not require an account or registration.
  • The app does not track your activity or usage.
  • The app does not use analytics, advertising, or third-party tracking services.
  • Any content or preferences within the app are stored locally on your device only and never leave it.

Third Parties

We do not share any information with third parties, because we do not collect any.

Children's Privacy

Storytime does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children.

Changes to This Policy

If our practices ever change, we will update this policy and revise the "last updated" date at the top of this page.

Contact

If you have any questions about this policy, contact us at hello@compution.co.uk.
